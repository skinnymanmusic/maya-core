# PRODUCT_STRATEGY.md  
## MayAssistant Product & Market Strategy (v1.2)

Last Updated: 2025-01-27

---

# 1. VISION

MayAssistant =  
**Your AI booking assistant for ANY appointment-based business.**

A single horizontal platform that feels vertical-specific without ever splitting the product.

---

# 2. PRIMARY MARKETS (2025)

### BEAUTY & WELLNESS  
- 400k–1.5M professionals  
- Biggest opportunity  
- Low dev cost  
- SMS-first flows  
- $79/mo

### EVENTS (DJs, AV)
- 50–100k pros  
- Already 70–85% built  
- High-value bookings  
- Payment deposits matter  
- $99/mo

---

# 3. PRICING STRATEGY

Beauty: $79/mo  
Events: $99/mo  
Founding members lock pricing forever.

ROI for Beauty:
- One saved appointment ≈ covers monthly cost

ROI for Events:
- Less than 5% of a typical booking

---

# 4. LAUNCH STRATEGY

1. **Pilot testing**  
2. **Founding members**  
3. **Landing pages for each vertical**  
4. **Social proof via family (wife, aunts)**  
5. **Gradual scaling**  

---

# 5. BRAND STORY

You built this for:
- your wife (nail tech)  
- your aunts (stylists)  
Then demand grew → platform expanded.

Authentic, honest, relatable.

---

# 6. COMPETITIVE MOAT

- Horizontal platform (vs Booksy/Honeybook)  
- Shared intelligence  
- Guardian Framework  
- Multi-tenant & encrypted  
- Fast iteration due to single codebase  

---
